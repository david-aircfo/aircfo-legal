Privacy Policy

airCFO Airbyte Integration  ·  Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how airCFO ("we," "us," or "our") handles data accessed through the airCFO Airbyte Integration application ("the App"). This App is a private, internal integration used solely by airCFO to synchronize QuickBooks Online financial data into airCFO's internal data infrastructure.

This App is not available to the public and is not listed on the Intuit App Store. It is operated exclusively for airCFO's internal business operations.

Data We Access

Through the QuickBooks Online API, the App accesses the following data from airCFO's own QuickBooks Online account:

  • Invoice records and line item details
  • Customer names and contact information
  • Product and service item descriptions
  • Payment records and transaction history

This data is accessed solely to support internal financial reporting and analysis at airCFO.

How We Use the Data

Data accessed through this App is used exclusively for:

  • Internal business intelligence and financial reporting
  • Loading data into airCFO's private BigQuery data warehouse
  • Supporting airCFO's operational decision-making

We do not use this data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose beyond internal operations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share data accessed through this App with any third parties. All data remains within airCFO's private internal infrastructure. Access is restricted to authorized airCFO personnel only.

Data Storage & Security

Data is stored in airCFO's Google Cloud BigQuery environment, which is protected by Google Cloud's enterprise security controls. Access to this data warehouse is governed by airCFO's internal access control policies.

OAuth credentials (Client ID, Client Secret, Access Token, and Refresh Token) are stored securely within airCFO's internal systems and are never shared externally.

Data Retention

Financial data synced through this App is retained in accordance with airCFO's internal data retention policies and applicable accounting record-keeping requirements. We retain data only as long as necessary for business and compliance purposes.
